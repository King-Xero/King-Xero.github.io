About Me

Hey there! I'm Toyan, a programmer pursuing a career in games development. I began my adventure into the world of video games when I was 3 years old, with the ever brilliant Super Mario World. After that I became obssessed and played anything and everything I could get my hands on. As I got a little older I decided I wanted to to make things for myself, so I began learning to code. Eventually I went on to study a bachelor's degree in computer games technology and after graduating I landed myself a software development role at Rock Solid Knowledge. I learned a great deal in my 4 years there. The shared knowledgebase made me a better developer, but the time, effort and care invested in me truly made me a better person. In late 2019 I decided to leave to study a master's degree in video game development. I'm now actively looking for my next great oppurtunity, and look forward to continuing my journey.
My Experience:
- 4 years of software development experience in an Agile, test driven environment
- Hold a bachelor's degree in computer games technology
- Currently studying a master's degree in video game development
