About Me

A bit about myself, my background, and my experience.

Hey there! I'm Toyan, a programmer currently working in the games industry. I began my adventure into the world of video games when I was 3 years old, with the ever-brilliant Super Mario World. After that, I became obsessed and began playing anything and everything I could get my hands on. As I got a little older I decided that I wanted to make things for myself, so I began learning to code.

Ultimately, I went on to study a Bachelor's degree in Computer Games Technology and after graduating I landed myself a software development role at Rock Solid Knowledge. I learned a great deal in my 4 years there. The shared knowledge base made me a better developer, but the time, effort and care invested in me truly made me a better person.

In late 2019 I decided to leave to continue my pursuit of knowledge by studying a Master's degree in Video Game Development. Leaning on my previous experience, I frequently excelled on the course and managed to land a job several months before finishing.

As of mid 2020, I have been working as a programmer on the game team at Codemasters Cheshire. I worked on Dirt 5 on the run up to launch and through it's service plan.
